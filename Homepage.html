Introduction about Malaysia


Malaysia is a unique country because Malaysia is a rich mixture of cultures, modern buildings next to traditional spice markets, and rainforests teeming with rare and endangered species. The Malaysian diversity of ethnicities and religions is celebrated throughout the year with many different multicultural festivals. Because Malaysia's population is so diverse, Malaysian people wiil organize open houses in conjunction with festive occasions such as Hari Raya Aidilfitri, Chinese New Year, and Deepavali. The organization of open houses allows Malaysian to enjoy various types of food from different ethnicities. This is because open houses typically involve the preparation of food from various cultures and traditions, including Malay, Chinese, Indian, and other ethnic foods found in Malaysia. The organization of open houses also can help to strengthen unity among races and relationships within society.
