About Us

Our Story

This food blog talks about all our love and passion in Malaysian food, beverages, and dessert. We would like to share various Malaysian food that we love since we always like to explore new flavors throughout Malaysia, our beloved country.

Our Mission

Malaysian cuisine is renowned for its rich history and diverse flavours due to its multicultural influences of all the races in Malaysia. Thus, our mission is to share and introduce Malaysian cuisine to people all around the world. We believe that food will bring people together that go beyond language and cultural barriers. It is important for us to be grateful that our beautiful country of origin has a wide variety of delectable cuisines for us to enjoy.
